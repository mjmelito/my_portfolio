Bio

Matt is a designer, web developer, and music producer based in Portland, Oregon. Having spent over a decade working in hospitality, he has a keen ability to anticipate the wants and needs of consumers. As a sommelier, he's used to synthesizing large amounts of information and later recalling that information to improve the guest experience on the spot.

Recognizing the growing need for thoughtful programmers as we increasingly rely on technology to enhance real world experience, Matt hopes to bring a user centered hospitality to his work as a web developer. His creativity and eye for design allow him to create applications that are not only intuitive, but also a pleasure to use.
