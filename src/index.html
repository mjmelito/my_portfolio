About Me

Resourceful and creative hospitality professional working to transition into a career in software development. Having spent close to a decade working in a variety of customer service roles, I'm well-practiced at anticipating consumer needs and quickly problem-solving to meet those needs when under pressure. I work best when I'm challenged with a complex task, and I'm used to working in a fast-paced, constantly changing environment. Earlier this year I began studying to become a full-stack web developer with Epicodus, where I've further honed my communication and critical thinking skills. As we continue to integrate technology with every aspect of our lives, I'm working to apply my hospitality experience to creating web applications that are efficient, equitable, and enjoyable to use. Check out my Github to see the projects I have worked on: https://github.com/mjmelito.
